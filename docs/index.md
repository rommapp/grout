## Features

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **Wireless Game Downloads**

    ---

    Browse and download games directly from your RomM library to your device over Wi-Fi.

-   **Box Art**

    ---

    Automatically download cover art so your frontend displays artwork for every game.

-   **BIOS Management**

    ---

    Download and verify BIOS files from your RomM server to the correct location on your device.

-   **Save Sync**

    ---

    Keep your game saves synchronized between your RomM server and your device. Play on one device, pick up on another.

-   **On-Device Platform Mapping**

    ---

    Map RomM platforms to the ROM directories on your device so Grout knows where to find and place your games.

-   **Multilingual**

    ---

    Available in English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, and Japanese.
